What FireWatch Does

A real-time operational readiness score based on your sleep, HRV, heart rate, and call load.

How do I connect my wearable device?

FireWatch reads data from Apple Health (iOS) or Google Health Connect (Android). Open the app, go to the Connect Device screen, and tap "Connect Apple Health" or "Connect Health Connect." Your wearable must already be syncing to one of these platforms.

My Garmin watch isn't showing HRV data — why?

Garmin does not natively sync HRV data to Apple Health or Google Health Connect. To get full HRV-based readiness scoring, download the free app Health Sync from the App Store or Play Store, which bridges your Garmin HRV data into Apple Health or Health Connect.

How do I create or join a crew?

Go to the Crew tab in the app. Tap "Create Crew" to start a new crew and get a shareable join code. Share that code with your crew members so they can join by tapping "Join Crew" and entering the code.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to the Profile tab → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account." This permanently deletes all your data from our servers and your device. This action cannot be undone. You can also request deletion by emailing firewatchtravis@gmail.com.

Is my health data shared with anyone?

No. Your health data is never sold or shared with third parties. It is used only to calculate your readiness scores within the app. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What wearables are supported?

Any wearable that syncs to Apple Health (iOS) or Google Health Connect (Android) is supported. This includes Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, Oura Ring, Polar, Whoop, and most other major fitness wearables. Note that some devices may not share all data types — see our connect screen for compatibility details.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. To cancel, go to your device's subscription settings and cancel FireWatch. You will retain access to premium features until the end of your current billing period.

Is FireWatch a medical device?

No. FireWatch is a personal wellness and operational readiness tool. It is not a medical device and should never be used to make fitness-for-duty decisions. Always follow your department's policies for duty-related decisions.
