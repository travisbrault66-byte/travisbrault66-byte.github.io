2. Description of the App

FireWatch is a personal wellness and operational readiness application designed for firefighters and emergency responders. The App combines wearable health data (heart rate, HRV, sleep, activity) with shift and call data to provide recovery scores, readiness insights, burnout risk indicators, and performance trends.

FireWatch is available in two tiers:

  • Free tier — access to core features at no cost
  • Premium subscription — access to advanced features, detailed analytics, and enhanced insights for a recurring fee
